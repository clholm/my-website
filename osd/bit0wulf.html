The bit0wulf is a DIY chiptune synth that uses keyboard switches and an Arduino Mega 2560 Rev3 to command two AY-3-8912 chips. It is currently in progress, (I still need to perform a first assembly) but you can view a sneak-peek of some designs below!

bit0wulf board 1 PCB (contains the AY-3-8912 chips, the audio out section, and is also an Arduino Mega 2560 Rev 3 shield)
